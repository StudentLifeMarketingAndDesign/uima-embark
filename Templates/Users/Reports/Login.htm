To create new portfolios:

  1. Find a selection of objects by using:
    • Index Tree - click the 'Collections' link in the left menu bar to navigate through portfolios
    • Quick Search - type a term in the Quick search box at the top of the left menu bar, then click
    • Advanced Search - click the 'Search' link in the left menu bar
  2. Click 'Add to portfolio'. (This link will only appear after you have a selection of object records.)
  3. Follow the directions on that page to add your selection to a new or existing portfolio.
To view or edit existing portfolios, click the 'My Portfolios' link in the left menu bar.

{if (Can Share)}

To share existing portfolios with the public:

  1. Click the 'My Portfolios' link in the left menu bar.
  2. Click the Properties link for the portfolio you would like to share.
  3. Mark Shared? as Yes and click Save.
